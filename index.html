How we prove a model

Five gates, every one published. No source → not in the corpus. No beat-base receipt → no download.

  1. Corpus, cited. Every fact traces to an authoritative source. No source → not in the corpus.
  2. Recipe, published. Base model, data, and settings — reproducible.
  3. Beat base or kill. The tuned model must beat its base on a held-out, deterministic eval. If it doesn't, it doesn't ship — and we say so.
  4. Receipt. The eval result is recorded honestly, win or lose, in the open cook ledger.
  5. Host. Only then does the model go up for download and flow down to your devices.

Why this way

"The AI trust-me-bro is over." A model you can't verify is a model you shouldn't trust. We show the corpus, the math, and the receipt so you never have to take our word for it.
